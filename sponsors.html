Sponsors


Our programs would not have been possible without the generous support of our sponsors. Our sponsors are not just organizations that support us financially, they are also volunteers who support us by sharing their valuable time, knowledge and enthusiasm. Every donation is significant, because it helps us achieve our goal of inspiring girls to become our future leaders and innovators. Potential sponsors should also be aware of the numerous benefits to themselves as well, as they profit from a diverse and growing STEM workforce in the years to come. If you are interested in sponsoring us in any way, please contact us at info@destinationSTEM.org.
